About

As a doctoral researcher at TU Wien's Atominstitut, I specialize in quantum force metrology with a focus on Casimir force measurements and quantum vacuum interactions. My work bridges theoretical quantum physics with cutting-edge experimental techniques, developing precision instruments that push the boundaries of measurement science.

My research combines expertise in laser systems, interferometry, and vacuum technology to explore fundamental questions in quantum gravity and non-Newtonian force detection. Through innovative experimental design and precision engineering, I contribute to our understanding of quantum field theory in confined geometries.
