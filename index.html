About Me

I'm a Doctoral Researcher at TU Wien's Atominstitut and a VCQ Fellow, specializing in Casimir force metrology and quantum vacuum interactions. My research focuses on precision measurements at the quantum level, exploring fundamental physics with potential applications in next-generation sensing technologies.

With expertise in experimental physics, nanoscale force detection, and optical metrology, I bridge the gap between fundamental quantum research and practical applications in sensing and measurement technology.
